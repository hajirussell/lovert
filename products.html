Basic Package

With the Basic Package, we send a sales representative to your nearest geographical location. It is up to you to make it to their traveling office, and, depending on your longitude, might require the use of climbing equipment. Our representative will provide the comfort of an office space if you require such mortal luxuries, beverages, light snacks, and all the paperwork necessary to book the vacation of your dreams.

There is no location that is off-limits, though, for our first-time clients, we suggest the Mysterious Flesh Pit or the Liminal Space Emporium.

We will walk you through the process of booking your own flight and hotel, though if you are interested in our flight and hotel services, please see the Gold and Platinum Packages for details.

This package includes ground and boat based transportation, a variety of food packages for families of all sizes, discount vouchers for local attractions, and a personal photographer to capture every waking moment of your getaway. And you’ll be saying getaway a lot by the time your vacation is over. Trust us.

If you are interested in the Basic Package, please submit a contact request from the Contact tab and our representatives will reach out.

Gold Package

With the Gold Package, you get everything from the Basic Package and more! Our representative will arrive at a location of your choosing, at your schedule. Office space will be provided, as well as a full-course meal, beverages, and the necessary paperwork with no need for climbing equipment. The vacation of your dreams and innermost desires will be booked at your discretion.

Our bespoke flight services also become available to you. You will be able to travel without the hassle of checking in on a commercial flight, and you will be provided fine sleeping quarters on our custom fleet of Bombardier Challenge 350s.

But the transportation options don’t stop there. You will also be provided with ground, boat, and helicopter transportation once you arrive at your final destination. All private, all the time. A second personal photographer is available as well, though they may not always be noticed. This is normal.

In addition to the stellar discounts of the Basic Package, you will also have access to exclusive VIP events at a number of locations around the world. Rest assured, there will be something available for you to participate in, no matter how remote it seems.

If you are interested in the Gold Package, please submit a contact request from the Contact tab and our representatives will reach out.

Platinum Package

If going Gold is not your style, our Platinum Package covers every possible need you had, have, and will have for as long as you remain a living and breathing creature on this green earth. We will arrive in your home, as soon as physically possible to wait on your every desire on hand and foot. All manner of food and drink will be provided in excess to ensure you get your fill. Maybe even more than that if that is what you wish.

Along with our bespoke flight services, you will also be provided five-star hotel treatment. You will never be without for a single moment. Our photographers will be plentiful. You will fawned over so much so that you may be mistaken for a celebrity in more densely populated areas.

Onlookers will become a part of the experience. Your vacation getaway will rival that of royalty, and you will not be able to look away from all the decadent offerings that will come to you. With the height of luxury at your fingertips, you will not want to leave. You will not be able to leave.

If you are interested in the Platinum Package, please submit a contact request from the Contact tab and our representatives will reach out at the soonest possible moment, so do not delay. Book your Lovert sponsored vacation today!
