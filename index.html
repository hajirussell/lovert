Welcome to Lovert

Your home away from home.

Started in 1923, in the heart of Massachusetts, Lovert has been spearheading existential experiences across the globe for the vacationer who just cannot take it anymore. Our professional and highly skilled team of globetrotting experts is here to ensure that your next vacation will be perfect from the moment of your conception to the moment you retire.

With offices available on every continent, there isn’t a single place we can’t go to serve you where you are. Our mission is to create the experiences of your dreams even if you do not quite know what you most desire at this moment.

Like international travel, the journey is the best part!
